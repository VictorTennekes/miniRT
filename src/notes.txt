primary rays: first rays to cast into the scene, finding objects and light.
secondary rays: shadow for example

Ray information:
	* starts at camera
	* camera by convention 1 unit behind aperture


raster space (0, 0) to (res_x, res_y)
NDC space (0, 0) to (1, 1)
Screen space (-1, 1) to (1, -1)

Image Aspect Ratio:
	ImageWidth / ImageHeight

fit screen space:
	x_coord * (ImageWidth / ImageHeight)

PixelNDCx = (Pixelx + 0,5) / imagewidth
PixelNDCy = (Pixely + 0,5) / Imageheight

PixelScreenx = 2 * PixelNDCx - 1
PixelScreeny = 2 * PixelNDCy - 1

PixelCamerax = (2 * PixelScreenx- 1) * ImageAspectRatio
PixelCameray = (1 - 2 * PixelScreeny)

sphere
	(x -c)^2 = r^2
	c = center
	r = radius

line:
	x = o +td
	o = origin
	d = direction
	t = parameter

intersection:
	(o -c -+td)^2 = r^2

	v = o - c

	v^2 + 2(v.d)t + d^2t^2 = r^2
